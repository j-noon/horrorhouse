About the game!

The idea of this game is to find your way through the room's to escape. To escape the Horror House you will need to find the token thats hidden within the game page and input it into the token field box. There is also a extra token to be found for the players who wish to find "ALL" of Gumpies treasure. Whilest playing and trying to figure out said puzzles you will come across educational content for you to follow and take in.
